Church and Kuo Group Joint Meetings
Wednesdays, Varian 208, Noon to 1 PM
A venue to discuss research topics we're working on and of current interest to the CMB community

 Ed's Ground rules:
1. Bring lunch. No one wants to see you go hungry.

2. Prepare to talk for about 10 to 15 minutes on either something you're currently working on or a current paper of your choice.

3. Slides are allowed if they contain only graphs, figures, pictures and equations. English words are allowed only on axis titles.

4. This is meant to foster discussion, ideas and better research, not to stress you out. Present things that are half-baked.

5. If it's not your week, bring questions about what you're working on. This is a great time to get the undivided attention of more experienced people on your particular problem.

6. Supplemental meetings discussing video games and the finer points of beer brewing will be held at Matt and Jamie's apartment.
